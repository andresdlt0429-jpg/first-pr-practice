FAQ

Questions people ask before booking.

What actually happens on the discovery call?

We'll look at your current website (if you have one), your service area, and where your competitors are outranking you. You'll leave with a clear picture of what's costing you jobs and what it would take to fix it — no slideshow, no hard pitch.

Do I need a brand-new website, or can you work with what I have?

It depends on what we find. Sometimes a handful of fixes to your existing site solves the problem. Other times a rebuild is the faster path. We'll tell you honestly which one you need — we don't push a rebuild if you don't need one.

How fast will I actually see results?

Most clients have their new site live within 2–3 weeks. Local SEO movement typically shows up within the first 60–90 days, and it keeps compounding from there — this is an ongoing system, not a one-and-done fix.

Do you lock me into a long-term contract?

No. Every plan is month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. We keep clients because the leads keep coming in, not because of a contract holding them there.

What if I'm already working with another marketing company?

That's fine — bring what they've built to the call. We'll give you a straight read on what's working, what isn't, and whether switching actually makes sense for your business.

I've worked with agencies before and got burned — how is this different?

Fair concern — a lot of trades have. Most agencies hand you a generic template, then disappear after month one with no real reporting. We only work with electricians and plumbers, so your site, keywords, and ad spend are built around your trade, not a one-size-fits-all package. You also get call tracking and lead-source reporting from day one, so you can see exactly what's working instead of taking our word for it.

How much does it cost to get started?

Plans start at $497/month for a new site build, hosting, and basic local SEO. We'll recommend the right tier for where your business is at right now on the call — no pressure to jump to the top package.
